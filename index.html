Tank cleaning tips

  • Make sure to test the water quality of the fish tank before proceeding to cleanse tank
  • With the proper cleaning materials and safety equiptment, proceed to clean algae out of tank
  • Ensure maximum tank cleanliness by purchasing the correct size tank for your fish collection
  • Clean your fish tank regularly to prevent bacteria from causing future tank problems
  • Feed your fish the appropriate amount of food and clean any excess food from water in order to keep it from sticking to the interior glass
  • If your tank contains live plants always remember to trim away dead or decaying leaves to prevent them from clogging the filter and sticking to glass
  • Vacuuming gravel in the bottom of the tank will ensure maximum cleanliness by getting rid of all decaying plants, food and other contaminents
